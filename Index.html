Welcome to My Bio!

As a committed Web Development student at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT), I am continually finding ways to interlace my academic journey with my profound love for music and sound. Being a drummer and a multi-talented musician, I've always found rhythm in everything I do, a trait that transcends into my technical endeavors. With a decade of experience in audio engineering under my belt, I have honed a meticulous ear for sound, a skill I am keen on melding with visual aesthetics. Now, with a desire to broaden my creative horizon, I am diving into the realms of design, videography, and photography. I am excited about the prospect of blending my auditory expertise with visual storytelling, as I decode the languages of the web. I am also embarking on a journey to capture life's resonance in a frame, orchestrating a melody of creativity that dances to the rhythm of imagination and innovation.
